Steps
1
Sort caps by color into piles. More variety = more vivid crab.
2
Draw a crab outline on plywood, marking zones for body, claws, legs.
3
Paint caps in batches — reds/oranges for body, dark shades for claws.
4
Glue each cap firmly cap-side up, section by section. Dry 24 hrs.
5
Add hanging loop and show the world your masterpiece! 🦀
